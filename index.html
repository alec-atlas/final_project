Welcome to Golden!
Golden is a door-through-door transportation service tailored to the specific needs of olders adults. Book a ride by signing in above or by calling us at 847-962-6839.
Tell me more
Golden's mission is to increase the quality of life of older adults by providing safe, easy-to-use, and customizable transportation. Each rider is unique and our drivers are trained to provide high-touch services to ensure you safely get to where you need to go.
Riding with Golden is different
Here at Golden, we focus on building relationships and forming meaningful connections with our riders. Getting into a strangers car can be anxiety inducing. Golden offers increased transparency into each driver BEFORE you book a ride, allowing you to book a ride with a driver you are most comfortable with. Although all of drivers have been vetted and expertly trained, we encourage you to ride with a Golden driver you've formed a connection with.
We also recognize some of our riders may need extra help. Golden is a door-through-door transportation service, which means we make sure each rider safely gets in and out of their residence, the car, and their destination. Lean on Golden for that extra support you may need.
Safety is important to us. We require each driver to list an emergency contact and, if requested, we will send ride notifications to this contact so friends and family know their loved ones are safe.
